About our school

At the Information Technology High School, we learn to turn our love for computers into a profession.
Our school recognizes that Information Technologies are the future, and it helps us gain the skills necessary to work in various fields of computer science and software design.

Programs and education

The primary goal of our school is to educate the next generation of programmers. Our school offers various programs for various specializations in different fields of computer science. With a team of 50 dedicated professors, there is something to learn for anybody here!

Extracurricular activities

Knowledge is power, but it is unfortunately not the only key to success! Besides our school offering us practical 21st-century skills, our school also offers the most diverse program of extracurricular activities out of any school in the region. ITHS supports us to form our personalities for the rest of our lives.

Clubs, workhops and after-school activities

There is no shortage of after-school activities at ITHS! The ITHS project center is the perfect place to gain real-life practical knowledge. It features work on real projects with real clients. Other activities include the Maths, Drama, Film, Science, Music, and Art clubs. Don't forget to check out the ITHS school band here!

Humanitarian work

Giving back is a core part of the curriculum at our school. It is incredibly important that we learn to help and listen to people of diverse backgrounds. We help where help is needed the most. Whether it be gifting books, school supplies, clothing, or just spending time with the elderly or children without parents, always a huge part in the spirit of the students.
